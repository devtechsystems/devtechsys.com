DevTech Sponsors 2018 SID-Washington Annual Conference

May 21, 2018

DevTech is a silver sponsor of the annual Society for International Development (SID) Washington Chapter conference, which will take place in Washington, DC, on May 31, 2018. The theme this year is “The Future of Development in a Rapidly Changing World,” and we are looking forward to discussing our vision of international development from the standpoint of data-driven, innovative solutions. The conference runs from 8 am to 7 pm at 1300 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW. We hope to see you there!
